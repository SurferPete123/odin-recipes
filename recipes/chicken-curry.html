Chicken Curry

Description

A very simple and tasty spicy chicken curry recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds skinless,boneless chicken breast
  • 1 whole chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in frying pan to medium to high heat,cook chicken until both sides are browned then set aside on a plate
  2. Put heat on medium and fry onion in oil, cook and stir until onions are cooked then add curry powder and cayenne pepper and continue to stir with onions
  3. Add chicken back into skillet with onion, curry powder and cayenne pepper, cover the skillet and simmer until chicken is no longer pink and looks juicy
