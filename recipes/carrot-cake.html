Carrot Cake

Description

Perfect Carrot Cake dessert to enjoy after dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup flaked coconut
  • 2 cups alll-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 teasppon salt
  • 2 cups grated carrot
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 cups confectioners'sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degress C.Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.Set aside
  2. In a large bowl, mix together sugar,oil, egss, vanilla and buttermilk.Stir in carrots, coconut andvanilla.In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt; gently stir into carrot mixture.Spread batter into prepared pan.
  3. Bake for 55 minutes or until toothpick inserted into cake comes out clean. Remove from oven, and set aside to cool.
  4. In a medium mixing bowl, combine butter or margarine, vanilla, and confectioners sugar. Blend until creamy. Frost cake while still in the pan.
